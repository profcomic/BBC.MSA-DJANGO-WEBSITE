BBC Youth Force

"Youthful Stage in His Presence"

The Mandate: Present, Purposeful, Powerful

The Youth Fellowship is designed for young adults and teenagers navigating the critical stages of life—from academic pressures and career decisions to identity, relationships, and mental wellness. We believe this "Youthful Stage" is not a waiting room but a powerful platform for spiritual influence.

Our mission is to equip a generation that is unapologetically **present** in their spiritual life, clear on their **purpose** in the world, and **powerful** in their faith, leveraging digital and social spaces for Kingdom impact. This is where faith meets culture, technology, and real life.

We don't just consume culture; we shape it, all while firmly rooted in His presence.

Kingdom Impact: Outreach & Online

Bold Outreach Initiatives

We are the hands and feet of Christ, dedicated to practical action that demonstrates God's love to the marginalized and underserved in our city.

  • **Street Ministry:** Food and blanket distribution to the homeless.
  • **Mentoring Youth:** Tutoring and mentorship programs for younger students.
  • **Crisis Relief:** Mobilizing quickly to assist with local disaster relief efforts.

Active Online Presence

Our presence online is strategic and intentional. We create a digital community that is positive, challenging, and spiritually feeding.

  • **Weekly Live Q&A:** Addressing challenging faith questions on Instagram/TikTok.
  • **Curated Content:** Sharing daily devotionals and theological deep-dives.
  • **Global Prayer Chain:** Using secure messaging apps for instant prayer requests.
