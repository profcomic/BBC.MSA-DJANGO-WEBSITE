"Reaching the Lost"

Ministry Overview

The BBC Outreach Ministry is passionate about fulfilling the Great Commission by taking the Gospel beyond the church walls to reach the lost, serve the needy, and transform communities with the love of Christ.

Through evangelism, community service, missions, and compassionate outreach programs, we demonstrate God's love in practical ways while sharing the message of salvation. We believe every believer is called to be a witness for Christ.

Our Mission

Evangelize

To share the Gospel with the lost and lead them to salvation in Jesus Christ

Serve Communities

To meet practical needs and demonstrate God's love through compassionate service

Support Missions

To partner with missionaries and support global mission efforts

What We Do

Street Evangelism

Taking the Gospel to the streets, markets, and public places to reach the unreached

Community Feeding Programs

Providing meals and food assistance to the homeless, orphans, and vulnerable families

Medical Outreach

Organizing free medical camps and health screenings in underserved communities

Prison & Hospital Visits

Ministering to inmates, patients, and those in institutions with the message of hope

School & Campus Outreach

Reaching students with the Gospel through campus fellowships and school programs

Mission Trips

Organizing local and international mission trips to spread the Gospel

Benevolence Ministry

Providing assistance to widows, orphans, and those facing financial hardship
