Men's Fellowship

"When Men Lift Their Holy Hands"

Anchoring Strength in God's Purpose

Welcome to the BBC Men's Fellowship, where we anchor our strength not just in the world's pursuits, but in God's purpose. Our mantra, "When Men Lift Their Holy Hands," is our calling: a commitment to spiritual leadership, integrity in the home, and profound impact in the community. We believe true manhood is defined by a Christ-centered character—a challenging, continuous journey that requires authenticity, accountability, and empowerment.

We are committed to guiding men through focused, interactive experiences designed to equip, refine, and activate the Godly potential within, moving every man from passive presence to decisive, spiritual leadership.

Activating The Man of God

The Covenant & Character Sessions

Monthly sessions focused on in-depth biblical teaching and challenging dialogue on faith, family, and finance. These are not lectures—they are interactive platforms for Iron sharpening iron.

Brotherhood Expeditions & Retreats

Annual retreats and invigorating outdoor trips designed to pull us away from distraction, forge unbreakable bonds of loyalty, and deepen spiritual resolve through shared experiences.

Impact and Mentorship Program

Moving from discussion to demonstration. We engage in intentional community service and mentorship initiatives, guiding the next generation and modeling compassion and responsibility.
