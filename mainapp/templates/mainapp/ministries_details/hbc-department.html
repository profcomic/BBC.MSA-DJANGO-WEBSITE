HBC DEPARTMENT

"Building Strong Communities"

Overseen by Rev Moses

Department Overview

The HBC (Home-Based Care) Department is committed to bringing the church to the community through small groups, home fellowships, and neighborhood outreach. We believe that authentic Christian community happens best in smaller, intimate settings where deep relationships are formed.

Under the oversight of Rev Moses, we organize and support home-based cells throughout the region, strategically divided into three geographical areas: Jerusalem HBC, Emmaus HBC, and Judea HBC. Each area creates spaces where believers can grow spiritually, support one another, and reach their neighbors with the Gospel.

Our Mission

Foster Fellowship

To create intimate communities where believers can connect and grow together

Disciple Believers

To provide discipleship and spiritual growth in home-based settings

Reach Neighborhoods

To evangelize and minister to communities through local cell groups

Provide Care

To offer pastoral care and support through community-based ministry

Our HBC Geographical Areas

Jerusalem HBC

Coverage Area: Milango Saba area down to Kadzonzo, stretching to Kajiweni and all areas near the church diameter.

The Jerusalem HBC serves the immediate community around BBC, providing accessible fellowship and discipleship for those living in close proximity to the church.

Emmaus HBC

Coverage Area: From Milango Saba and beyond, stretching to Mishomoroni, Freretown, and surrounding areas.

The Emmaus HBC extends our reach to the broader Mombasa community, bringing the church to neighborhoods and creating vibrant home fellowships in these areas.

Judea HBC

Coverage Area: Those around Vikwatani, Mtopanga area, outside of Kisauni, and far beyond.

The Judea HBC serves our members and believers in distant locations, ensuring that no one is too far to experience the fellowship and spiritual support of our church family.

What We Do

Home Cell Groups

Weekly gatherings in homes for Bible study, prayer, and fellowship

Discipleship Training

Structured programs for spiritual growth and leadership development

Prayer Networks

Coordinated prayer chains and intercession across all cell groups

Community Outreach

Neighborhood evangelism and service projects organized by cell groups

Cell Leader Training

Equipping and empowering cell leaders with tools and resources

Social Events

Organizing fellowship events, celebrations, and community gatherings

Pastoral Care

Providing support during life challenges through cell group networks
