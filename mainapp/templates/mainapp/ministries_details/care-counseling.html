Ministry Overview

The BBC Care and Counseling Ministry is dedicated to providing spiritual, emotional, and practical support to individuals and families facing life's challenges. We believe that God's love brings healing and restoration to broken hearts.

Under the leadership of our pastoral team, we offer biblical counseling, crisis intervention, pastoral care, and support groups to help people navigate difficult seasons and experience God's transforming power in their lives.

Our Mission

Provide Biblical Counseling

To offer Christ-centered counseling grounded in Scripture for life's challenges

Bring Emotional Healing

To minister healing to those dealing with trauma, grief, and emotional pain

Support Families

To strengthen marriages and families through godly counsel and support

Walk Alongside

To provide compassionate pastoral care during times of crisis and transition
