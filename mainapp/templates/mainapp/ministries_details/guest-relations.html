GUEST RELATIONS DEPARTMENT

"First Impressions Matter"

Led by Rev Odanga

Department Overview

The Guest Relations Department is the welcoming face of BBC, ensuring that every visitor experiences the warmth and love of Christ from the moment they arrive. We believe that first impressions are lasting impressions.

Under the leadership of Rev Odanga, our team is trained to provide exceptional hospitality, making every guest feel valued, comfortable, and eager to return. We create an atmosphere where people encounter God's love through genuine care.

Our Mission

Welcome Warmly

To greet every guest with genuine warmth and Christ-like hospitality

Create Comfort

To ensure guests feel comfortable and at ease in our church environment

Provide Information

To answer questions and guide guests to the resources they need

Foster Connection

To help guests connect with the church family and find their place

What We Do

Guest Reception

Welcoming guests at the entrance with warm greetings and friendly smiles

Information Services

Providing information about church programs, events, and ministries

Welcome Packages

Preparing and distributing welcome gifts and information packets for first-time visitors

Follow-Up Ministry

Reaching out to guests after their visit to answer questions and invite them back

Guest Registration

Managing guest registration and maintaining visitor records for follow-up

Facility Navigation

Helping guests navigate the church facility and find specific locations

Special Needs Assistance

Providing support for guests with special needs or accessibility requirements
