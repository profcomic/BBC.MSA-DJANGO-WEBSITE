Ministry Overview

The Young Professionals Training Ministry is designed to equip young professionals with the spiritual foundation, practical skills, and godly character needed to excel in their careers while maintaining their Christian witness.

We believe that the marketplace is a mission field, and young professionals are called to be salt and light in their workplaces. Through training, mentorship, and fellowship, we prepare them to impact their industries for Christ.

Our Mission

Develop Leaders

To raise up godly leaders who will influence their workplaces and industries

Build Skills

To provide practical training in leadership, entrepreneurship, and professional excellence

Foster Integrity

To instill biblical values and integrity in all professional endeavors

Create Networks

To build a community of like-minded professionals for support and collaboration
