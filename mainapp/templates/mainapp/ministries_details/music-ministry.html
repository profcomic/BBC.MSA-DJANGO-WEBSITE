Ministry Overview

Under the leadership of Pastor Kinzi, the BBC Praise & Worship Ministry is dedicated to leading God's people into His presence through anointed worship and praise. We believe that music is a powerful tool for worship, evangelism, and spiritual transformation.

Our ministry encompasses various musical expressions including the choir, praise team, instrumentalists, and technical support, all working together to create an atmosphere where God is glorified and hearts are touched.

Our Mission

Lead Worship

To usher believers into the presence of God through Spirit-led worship and praise

Develop Talent

To nurture and develop musical gifts and talents for God's glory

Create Excellence

To maintain excellence in all musical presentations as an offering to God

Minister Healing

To bring healing, deliverance, and breakthrough through prophetic worship

Our Teams

Praise & Worship Team
