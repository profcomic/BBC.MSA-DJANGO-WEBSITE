Welcome to BBC Marriage Connect

Welcome to the Marriage Connect Department at BBC MINISTRIES. We believe that strong marriages are the foundation of thriving families and vibrant communities. Our mission is simple yet profound: to cultivate an environment where married couples can come together to learn, connect, and support one another on their lifelong journey of love and commitment.

At the heart of our ministry is the belief that marriage is a sacred covenant ordained by God, and it deserves to be nurtured, celebrated, and protected.

Our Commitment: Nurturing Your Covenant

Equip & Empower

Through workshops, seminars, and resources, we equip couples with practical tools and biblical insights to strengthen communication and resolve conflict with grace.

Cultivate Connection

We host retreats and social gatherings designed to help couples reignite romance, deepen intimacy, and prioritize quality time away from daily demands.

Family Foundation

Our focus extends beyond the couple to building godly families, rooted in shared faith and values, serving as a beacon of hope for the next generation.

Highlighted Programs & Enrichments

Annual Signature Event

The Covenant Retreat

An immersive weekend experience focusing on intensive spiritual and emotional connection. Through specialized teaching, focused couple sessions, and dedicated worship, this retreat is designed to reset your foundation and elevate your relationship to the next level of intimacy and purpose.

Connect & Fun

Quarterly Date Night Connect

Make time for romance! These themed evenings feature fine dining, interactive activities, and a short, powerful, and practical message on topics like financial harmony, intentional parenting, or re-igniting physical connection. Perfect for keeping the spark alive amidst a busy schedule.

Preparation & Foundation

Covenant Launch: Pre-Marital Mentorship

A rigorous, mentorship-based program designed for engaged couples. It covers critical areas including communication, expectations, conflict resolution, intimacy, and spiritual unity, ensuring a solid, Godly foundation for marriage before saying "I Do."

Learn & Act

Random Seminars & Conferences

Spontaneous, impactful learning events covering diverse topics from marriage foundations to daily spiritual growth. Each session offers practical tools and collaborative mentorship in critical areas like communication, conflict resolution, and financial unity. No fixed schedule—just timely opportunities to deepen your knowledge and put your faith into action.
