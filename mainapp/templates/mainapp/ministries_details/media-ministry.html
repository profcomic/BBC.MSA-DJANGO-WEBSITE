Ministry Overview

The BBC Media Ministry is committed to documenting, broadcasting, and amplifying the message of the Gospel through modern media technology. We capture the moments when God moves and share them with the world.

From live streaming services to creating engaging content for social media, photography, videography, and graphic design, we ensure that the Word of God reaches beyond the four walls of our church.

Our Mission

Broadcast the Gospel

To extend the reach of God's Word through digital platforms and media channels

Document God's Work

To capture and preserve testimonies, services, and special events for posterity

Engage Online

To create compelling content that engages believers and reaches the lost online

Support Ministry

To provide technical and creative support for all church programs and events

What We Do

Live Streaming

Broadcasting services live to reach members and viewers worldwide

Photography & Videography

Professional documentation of services, events, and special occasions

Graphic Design

Creating visual content for bulletins, social media, and promotional materials

Social Media Management

Managing church presence across Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and other platforms

Video Editing & Production

Post-production work to create polished, professional content
