Pastor Alfred Matai

Instruments Team Lead & Church Treasurer

Pastor Alfred Matai is a dedicated leader who contributes vital skills in both the instruments ministry and financial stewardship of Berean Ministries. His dual responsibilities ensure that the church's services are technically excellent and that its resources are managed with integrity.

As the Instruments Team Lead, Pst Alfred oversees the entire technical and musical infrastructure of the worship department. He is responsible for managing the equipment, mentoring musicians, and ensuring the sound and musical arrangements enhance the atmosphere of authentic worship, supporting the overall vision of the Praise and Worship Team.

In addition to his creative role, he handles the crucial function of the Church Treasurer. This role demands precision, transparency, and high integrity in managing the church's finances, offerings, and operational budget, ensuring the ministry remains accountable and financially healthy which he has so far shown the described levels.

In the marketplace, Pastor Alfred showcases the diversity of Christian talent by working in the beauty and therapy industry. This unique professional background allows him to connect with people on a personal level, reflecting the healing and restorative aspect of God's love outside of the traditional ministry setting. His life demonstrates that genuine service, whether spiritual or professional, brings beauty and wellness to the community.

Pastor Alfred is committed to excellence in all his endeavors, serving as a passionate and disciplined leader in every facet of the Berean Ministries family.
