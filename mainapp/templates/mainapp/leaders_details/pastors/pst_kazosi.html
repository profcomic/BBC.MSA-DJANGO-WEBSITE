Pastor Agnes Kazosi

HBC Pastor & Ministry Expansion Team Member

Pastor Agnes Kazosi is a highly valued and essential member of the pastoral team, known for her humble spirit, unwavering loyalty, and well-composed leadership**. She brings a powerful blend of spiritual maturity and practical organizational skills to Berean Ministries.

As a key leader in the pastoral team, Pastor Agnes focuses on discipleship and community building. Her strategic leadership has been instrumental in the church's expansion efforts, particularly in helping to lead the growing Jerusalem Home Bible Church (HBC) satellite ministry. She is passionate about ensuring every member feels connected, nurtured, and fully equipped for their personal ministry.

Pastor Agnes is defined by her energetic and industrious nature. Beyond the pulpit, she is a successful business lady, showcasing her entrepreneurial drive and proving that a life of deep faith can coexist with excellence in the marketplace. Her business acumen provides invaluable insight into stewardship and effective resource management within the ministry.

Her dedication is rooted in her family life. She is married to Mr. Nyamai Mutila and together they are raising four children. Their family embodies the dedication and stability that serve as a guiding example to the church community. Pastor Agnes is committed to serving Christ and His church with excellence in every role she holds.
