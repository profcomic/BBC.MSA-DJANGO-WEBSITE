Rev. Lorine P.A. Ang'ana

Bishop's Wife, BBC Minister, Founder - Women of Value

Rev. Lorine P.A. Ang'ana is a dynamic and highly influential leader, serving alongside her husband, Bishop Dr. Ibrahim A.O, as a Co-Founder of Berean Ministries. Her ministry is driven by a profound focus on the well-being and empowerment of women and youth.

She is the visionary Founder of the Women of Value (WOV) Interdenominational network, an impactful ministry dedicated to equipping women to discover their God-given purpose, operate in their gifts, and embrace godly character. This interdenominational focus extends her influence far beyond the local church.

Termed as MAMA VALUE, Rev. Lorine also serves in key regional leadership roles, including the Vice Chairlady of the Mombasa North Pastors Wives Fellowship. She is a sought-after keynote speaker at numerous women's conferences, recognized for her practical, Scripture-based messages that bring both spiritual and practical transformation.

Demonstrating her diverse skills, she provides vital leadership in the marketplace as the Managing Director of PalmGold Investments, the successful real estate and training corporation co-founded with her husband. Rev. Lorine P.A. is committed to developing leaders who impact both the church and the community at large.

She is also a family advocate, passionately supporting initiatives that strengthen families and promote healthy relationships. Her ministry is marked by a heart for service, a commitment to excellence, and a vision to see families thrive.

Rev Lorine & Bishop A.O are blessed with three wonderful children who are a gift to the body of christ.
