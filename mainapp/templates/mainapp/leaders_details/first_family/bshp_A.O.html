Bishop Ibrahim A.O

Founder & Overseer Berean Ministries

Bishop Dr. Ibrahim A.O is a dynamic leader, visionary entrepreneur, and devoted servant of God. Together with his wife, Rev Lorine P.A, they have dedicated their lives to founding and overseeing Berean Ministries,originally established in 2002 as Hallelujah Worship Centre Church impacting countless lives globally through spiritual guidance and community development.

Renowned for his expertise and leadership, he is a widely recognized entrepreneur and influential thinker, with a notable impact across East Africa and beyond. In 2005, they launched the PalmGold Investment, a highly successful real estate and training corporation that swiftly ascended to prominence as a frontrunner in the real estate sector Operating in over 10 Kenyan counties, the PalmGold Investment emerged as a powerhouse in the industry, amassing significant success.

He is also the founder of the Berean Leadership Institute, a premier leadership training institution dedicated to nurturing and empowering future leaders. His commitment to excellence and innovation is evident in the institute's comprehensive programs and initiatives. Futhermore, Bishop Ibrahim A.O is a sought-after speaker and mentor, frequently invited to share his insights at conferences and was once the president of eagle's rock interdenomination in kenya.

Presently, Ibrahim holds the position of legal advisor to Mombasa Pastors Fellowship Mombasa zone, among other ventures. Founded with the aim of spearheading housing developments throughout Africa, PalmGold Investment has successfully completed over 20 housing projects, with more than 10 additional projects currently underway. Bshp Ibrahim A.O is married to Rev Lorine P.A, and together they are proud parents of four children.
