Reverend Moses Oyoti

Departmental Overseer - Hallelujah Worship Sanctuary

Rev. Moses Oyoti stands as a steadfast pillar and one of the longest-serving pastors in Berean Ministries. His tenure reflects a deep, unwavering loyalty to the Apostolic vision established by the founders and a passion for mentoring the next generation of leaders. He provides essential stability and spiritual wisdom to the church body.

His ministry is characterized by a unique ability to connect complex spiritual truths with practical, everyday life. Rev. Moses is particularly focused on family discipleship and the integration of faith into the marketplace, demonstrating that God's power is relevant in every sphere of life. His quiet strength and consistent faithfulness have guided countless members through challenging seasons.

Professionally, Rev. Oyoti brings a wealth of specialized knowledge to the table as a highly qualified Boiler Engineer. This background in precision, diagnostics, and maintaining complex systems mirrors his meticulous approach to ministry and church stewardship. His success in this technical field serves as a powerful testimony to the congregation that excellence in the professional world is part of the Christian mandate.

Rev. Moses is committed to the foundation of the family, sharing his life and ministry with his dedicated wife, Pst Philgona Oyoti. Together, they are blessed with three children, modeling a life of integrity, service, and generational faithfulness that profoundly impacts the Berean community.
