Reverend Constance Odanga

Senior Pastor, Hallelujah worship Sanctuary

Rev. Odanga is a highly devoted and loyal servant of God, operating as the Senior Pastor of Hallelujah Worship Sanctuary. He is integral to the day-to-day spiritual and administrative functions of the church, ensuring faithful adherence to the Word and the Apostolic vision of the ministry.

Rev. Odanga serves as a key departmental leader, overseeing two vital arms of the church: the Intercessory Ministry (War Room), Jerusalem HBC and the Teens Ministry. His oversight ensures that both the powerful prayer foundation and the next generation of believers are strongly supported and nurtured.

Professionally, Rev. Odanga is an accomplished and highly qualified graphic designer who seamlessly blends his creative talent with his calling. He runs his own successful company, Bigyard Arts, demonstrating the ability of believers to excel in both ministry and the marketplace.

He is a dedicated family man, married to Mrs. Bridgit Okutoyi Odanga, and together they are blessed with three children. Their family's commitment provides a foundation of faithful service to the Berean Ministries community.
