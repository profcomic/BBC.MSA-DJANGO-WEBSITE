BEREAN BIBLE CHURCH

God's church with excellence.

BBC Mombasa is a community of believers committed
to living out the teachings of Christ. We believe that church is a Family
and Not a congregation. Ours is a family church that is concerned with
your well-being through prayer and mutual support of pastoral care
for all members.

The BBC Ministries have a God given mandate of Raising
Kingdom Citizens.These are the people who are deliberate on pursuing their
God-given purposes in their different areas of calling-whether in ministry or
in their personal lives.

Thanks for stopping by our website. Whether it's your
first visit or you're visiting our site again, we love that you're taking time
to visit online. We hope you find a great place to connect with our family.
